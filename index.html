About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2008, Carmel Bible College is dedicated to provide comprehensive biblical education in a supportive Christian environment. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and spiritual formation necessary for effective ministry and Christian service.

Carmel Bible College is proudly accredited by the International Association for Theological Accreditation (IATA), reflecting our commitment to maintaining high academic and spiritual standards in theological education.

We strive to be a premier biblical institution, known for academic excellence, spiritual vitality, and practical ministry preparation. Our graduates serve as pastors, missionaries, educators, and Christian leaders throughout the world.

Our Values

We are committed to:

  • Biblical Authority: We affirm the Bible as God's inspired and authoritative Word.
  • Academic Excellence: We provide rigorous theological education with high academic standards.
  • Spiritual Formation: We nurture spiritual growth through mentoring, prayer, and community life.
  • Practical Ministry: We prepare students for effective service through hands-on ministry experience.
  • Global Perspective: We embrace diverse cultural perspectives and equip students for global impact.

Master of Divinity (M.Div)

The Master of Divinity (M.Div) is a 3/2-year postgraduate degree designed to prepare students for pastoral ministry, teaching, and other church-related roles, focusing on biblical studies, theology, and ministry skills.
