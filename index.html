About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2008, Carmel Bible College is dedicated to providing comprehensive biblical education in a supportive Christian environment. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and spiritual formation necessary for effective ministry and Christian service.

We strive to be a premier biblical institution, known for academic excellence, spiritual vitality, and practical ministry preparation. Our graduates serve as pastors, missionaries, educators, and Christian leaders throughout the world.

Our Values

At Carmel Bible College, we are committed to:

  • Biblical Authority: We affirm the Bible as God's inspired and authoritative Word.
  • Academic Excellence: We provide rigorous theological education with high academic standards.
  • Spiritual Formation: We nurture spiritual growth through mentoring, prayer, and community life.
  • Practical Ministry: We prepare students for effective service through hands-on ministry experience.
  • Global Perspective: We embrace diverse cultural perspectives and equip students for global impact.
