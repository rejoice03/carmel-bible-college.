About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2008, Carmel Bible College is dedicated to providing comprehensive biblical education in a supportive Christian environment. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and spiritual formation necessary for effective ministry and Christian service.

Carmel Bible College is proudly accredited by the International Association for Theological Accreditation (IATA), reflecting our commitment to maintaining high academic and spiritual standards in theological education.

We strive to be a premier biblical institution, known for academic excellence, spiritual vitality, and practical ministry preparation. Our graduates serve as pastors, missionaries, educators, and Christian leaders throughout the world.

Our Values

At Carmel Bible College, we are committed to:

  • Biblical Authority: We affirm the Bible as God's inspired and authoritative Word.
  • Academic Excellence: We provide rigorous theological education with high academic standards.
  • Spiritual Formation: We nurture spiritual growth through mentoring, prayer, and community life.
  • Practical Ministry: We prepare students for effective service through hands-on ministry experience.
  • Global Perspective: We embrace diverse cultural perspectives and equip students for global impact.

Our Courses

M.Th Religion and Mission

The Master of Theology program is a two-year, post-graduate research program designed to deepen knowledge in theology, enhance skills in teaching and preaching, and equip students for leadership in various church ministries, including missions.

Qualification

BD./M.Div Duration 2 Years

Master of Divinity (M.Div)

The Master of Divinity (M.Div) is a 3/2-year postgraduate degree designed to prepare students for pastoral ministry, teaching, and other church-related roles, focusing on biblical studies, theology, and ministry skills.

Qualification

B.Th./University Degree Duration 3/2 Years

Bachelor of Theology (B.Th)

Bachelor of Theology (B.Th) course is a 3-year, ministry-oriented program that provides students with foundational knowledge of the Bible, interpretive skills, and world understanding for various ministries.

Qualification

Plus 2 Duration 3 Years

Language

English / Malayalam

Graduate in Theology" (G.Th)

The "Graduate in Theology" (G.Th) course is a three-year undergraduate Bible College degree program, designed to equip individuals with a deep understanding of the Bible and prepare them for pastoral ministry.

Diploma in Theology (D.Th)

Diploma in Theology (D.Th) is a two-year introductory course that prepares students for a Bachelor's Degree in Theology, focusing on foundational theological knowledge and ministry skills.

C.Th. (Certificate in Theology)

C.Th. (Certificate in Theology) The course is a one-year certificate program designed to provide a foundational theological education, typically for those seeking to engage in Christian ministry or lay leadership.
